## Full description

Dictionary is an Android app for learning vocabulary in a practical, repeatable way.

It helps you import word lists, organize them, listen to pronunciation, practice with focused exercises, and keep your own local word database for long-term study.

Main features:

- import words from bundled assets or text files
- save words into a local database
- filter words by language and topic
- listen to words and translations with configurable playback
- use text-to-speech with selectable voices
- practice with word matching exercises
- practice article matching
- order words in a sentence
- train with grammar-focused exercises
- adjust learning settings and save presets

The app is designed for people who want a flexible vocabulary trainer instead of a fixed course. You can study imported word lists, repeat difficult words, and adapt the training flow to your own learning style.

Dictionary includes sample learning content in the app assets and supports offline-oriented use for word review, filtering, and local storage.
