Privacy Policy for Dictionary

Last updated: April 7, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how the Dictionary Android application ("Dictionary", "the app") handles information when you use it.

Summary

Dictionary is a vocabulary learning application. The app is designed primarily for local use on your device. It does not require account registration and does not include built-in advertising, analytics, or user tracking.

Information the app may access

The app may access the following information or device capabilities:

  • Files you choose to open or export, when you import vocabulary from a text file or export words from the local database.
  • Locally stored app data, such as imported words, topics, knowledge values, presets, and settings saved on your device.

How the app uses this information

  • To import, display, edit, filter, and export vocabulary data.
  • To save your settings and learning progress locally on your device.
  • To provide text-to-speech playback and audio training features.

Data sharing

The app does not sell your personal data. The app does not include built-in advertising SDKs or analytics SDKs.

Data storage and retention

Vocabulary data, settings, and other app content are stored locally on your device unless you choose to export them. You can remove app data by clearing the app's storage or uninstalling the app. You can also delete imported words from inside the app where supported.

Children's privacy

The app is not directed specifically to children. If you believe the app has collected personal information in a way that should be addressed, contact the developer.

Security

The app is intended for offline/local use and aims to minimize data collection. However, no method of electronic storage or processing can be guaranteed to be completely secure.

Changes to this policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. The latest version should be published at the URL provided with the app listing.
